Introducing the YOASOBI band members

YOASOBI is a Japanese pop duo formed in 2019, consisting of composer Ayase and singer-songwriter Ikura. Their band members for live shows consists of Hikaru Yamamoto as the bassist, AssH as the guitarist, Honogumo as the drummer and Zakuro Misohagi as the keyboardist/backing vocals.

  • Ikura (Vocalist)
  • Ayase (Composer)
  • Hikaru (Bassist)
  • AssH (Guitarist)
  • Honogumo (Drummer)
  • Zakuro (Keyboardist)
